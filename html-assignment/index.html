About Me

I am studying Interactive Media Design (IMD))in Algonquin College. I have a background in Information Technology. As a child, My dream was to become an electronic genius. So, after high school, I continued my studying in electronics at university. In 2002, I received my bachelor's degree in Electronics Engineering, and in the same year, I started my master's in Opto-Electronic engineering. During that program, I was fascinated by quantum mechanics and I realized the hidden beauty in the famous E=mc2 equation. Inspired by Albert Einstein's lifestyle, I started learning the violin. I tried for 3 years but I was not much successful in that.

After graduation, I was hired by Iranian Oil Pipline and Telecomunication Company (IOPTC).
I work there for almost 10 years, first as a field engineer and next IT department administrator. During those years, I gained experience in working with optical devices, cisco switches, HP servers, and virtualization. After marriage, I decided to change my career and start my own business. Registering in college for a fresh start was the first step I made to make it possible.
